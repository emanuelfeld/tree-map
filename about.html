About

Wish there were more trees in your DC neighborhood?

Residents can suggest new planting locations to DDOT's Urban Forestry Administration until June 15th. UFA will then prioritize requested areas in its Fall planting season.

This site can help you investigate where there are available tree boxes near you.

Use the map page to search for your address, click on one of the open plots (shown as orange points), and use the link provided in the pop-up to submit a request through 311.dc.gov. Just copy-paste the address you see into the 311 form.

To help you describe the plot location in your request, you can check it out using the Google Street View link provided. Of course, you could also go look at it outside!

In the site's logo you'll find the outline of a leaf from a Sugar Maple tree, one of the most common in the District.
