About

Wish there were more trees in your DC neighborhood?

Residents can suggest new planting locations to DDOT's Urban Forestry Administration until June 15th. UFA will then prioritize requested areas in its Fall planting season.

Use this site to investigate where there are empty tree boxes near you.

Just search for your address, click on one of the orange points on the map, and use the link provided in the pop-up to begin submitting a request through 311.dc.gov.

Before asking for a new tree, double check that the space hasn't already been filled by walking over to the location or by checking it out using the Google Street View link.
